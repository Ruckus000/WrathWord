# Privacy Policy for WrathWord

**Last Updated: January 25, 2025**

## Introduction

WrathWord ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** - Used for account authentication and password recovery
- **Display name** - Used to identify you within the app and to friends
- **Friend code** - A unique identifier (format: XXXX-XXXX) for adding friends

### Game Data
We automatically collect:
- **Game statistics** - Including games played, win/loss records, guess counts, and streaks
- **Game history** - Records of daily and free play games completed

### Technical Data
- **Device information** - Basic device identifiers for app functionality
- **App usage data** - How you interact with game features

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the game service
- Enable account creation and authentication
- Display your statistics and leaderboard rankings
- Allow you to connect with friends via friend codes
- Improve the app experience

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## Data Sharing

**We do not:**
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use third-party analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements in the app

Your game statistics may be visible to users you have added as friends within the app.

## Data Retention

We retain your account information and game data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Export your game statistics

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

## Children's Privacy

WrathWord is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
